## Corpus sources

### USDA FoodData Central
Status: **Public domain (US Government Work)**.
Per 17 U.S.C. § 105, works of the US federal government are not subject to copyright protection. The USDA FDC explicitly states data is in the public domain and free to use.
Source URL: https://fdc.nal.usda.gov/

### EU Reglamento (UE) Nº 1169/2011
Status: **Free reuse with attribution (Commission Decision 2011/833/EU)**.
The European Commission's reuse policy applies. Attribution: "Source: European Union, https://eur-lex.europa.eu/, [year]".
Source URL: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/ES/TXT/?uri=CELEX:02011R1169-20180101

### Escoffier *Le Guide Culinaire* (Project Gutenberg edition)
Status: **Public domain (>100 years post mortem auctoris)**.
Auguste Escoffier died 1935; *Le Guide Culinaire* first published 1903. Public domain in jurisdictions with 70-year PMA terms (EU, US for pre-1929 works). Project Gutenberg edition further confirms public-domain status in the US.
Source URL: https://www.gutenberg.org/

### CIAA Spain materials
Status: **Permission required**.
Confederación de Industrias de Alimentación y Bebidas (CIAA Spain) materials are copyright © CIAA. Ingestion is gated on explicit written permission. Until granted, the script exits cleanly without downloading or ingesting any data.
Source URL: https://www.fiab.es/ (formerly ciaa.es)

## Modern cookbooks — explicitly out of scope

The following are **not ingested** and are filed in the follow-up slice `m2-ai-yield-cookbooks-modern` pending legal due diligence:

- *Larousse Gastronomique* (Hachette Livre, copyrighted)
- *The Professional Chef* (Culinary Institute of America / Wiley, copyrighted)
- *On Food and Cooking* (Harold McGee / Scribner, copyrighted)

Ingestion of any of these requires a publisher licensing agreement.
